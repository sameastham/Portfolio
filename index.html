ABOUT

A Lead UX designer with over eight years proven experience working in Fortune 500 companies. Sam has led UX design teams and projects in fast-paced, highly collaborative agile environments. As a lead designer, he has established best practices and design guidelines for company-wide application interfaces across multiple platforms, with the ability to adapt to shifting business priorities.

Sam has a track record of successful project delivery by integrating complex user flows, large data sets, and multiple user personas into intuitive user interfaces, working alongside business analysts, front-end developers, and business stakeholders at each stage in the design process from conception to launch. He strongly emphasizes the importance of research-backed, user-centered design in his work in combination with established usability principles and accessibility guidelines.
